# New Game Ideas

## Next Up

1. **Nonogram / Picross** — Grid-fill logic puzzles (like Picross/Hanjie). Numbers reveal pixel art images. Satisfying and original for the collection.

## Puzzle / Strategy

2. **Pipe Dream / Flow Puzzle** — Connect colored endpoints on a grid without crossing paths. Clean, minimalist design.

3. **Sliding Tile Puzzle** — 15-puzzle variant with hand-drawn or pixel art images that shuffle and reassemble. Could feature multiple difficulty levels.

## Arcade / Action

4. **Breakout / Arkanoid** — Brick-breaker with power-ups, multiple levels, and special brick types. Classic arcade genre not yet in the collection.

5. **Snake Evolution** — Classic snake but with power-ups, obstacles, enemy snakes, and a level progression system.

6. **Flappy-Style Endless Runner** — Infinite side-scroller with obstacles and a scoring system. Could have a unique theme (lava cavern, underwater, neon city).

7. **Asteroids Remix** — Physics-based space ship with momentum, breaking asteroids into smaller pieces, weapon unlocks.

## Platformer

8. **2D Platformer** — Side-scrolling platformer with 5–10 levels, enemies, collectibles, and simple physics. Noticeably absent from the current collection.

## Card / Board Games

9. **Solitaire / Patience** — Classic card game (Klondike or FreeCell) with animated card dealing and move validation.

10. **Blackjack / Card Battler** — Deck-building lite: pick cards each round to build a hand, beat escalating opponents. Bridges the RPG and card game genres.

## Simulation / Idle

11. **Idle Clicker / Cookie Clicker Clone** — Click to produce a resource, buy upgrades to automate and multiply production. Oddly satisfying and missing from the collection.

12. **City Builder Micro** — Small-scale tile placement city builder: place houses, roads, parks; track population and happiness. Turn-based or real-time.

## Other

13. **Memory / Simon Says** — Classic memory sequence game with increasing pattern length, sounds, and a leaderboard.

14. **Word Game (Wordle-like)** — 5-letter word guessing with colored feedback. Could have a themed word list (fantasy words, tech terms, etc.) to differentiate it.
